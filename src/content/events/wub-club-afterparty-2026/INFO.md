Join the Wub Club for a late-night experience sponsored by Minneapolis local <a href="https://www.facebook.com/profile.php?id=61565099339049" target="_blank">**SubLab Sound**</a>. Get your ticket now and sign up for the Party Bus to get a ride from the Wub Club Anniversary Special to the afters location at Oga Madam's African Kitchen. Duluth's <a href="https://www.haccircle.com/" target="_blank">**Human Artistic Collaborations (H.A.C.)**</a> will be providing eye-candy in the form of string art, live visuals, projection mapping, and even have an interactive photo booth for attendees!
